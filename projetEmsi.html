About EMSI

Engineering the Future Since 1986

EMSI is Morocco’s leading private engineering school, dedicated to excellence in science and innovation since its founding in 1986.

With campuses in Casablanca, Rabat, and Marrakech, EMSI provides hands-on training, high-tech labs, and a curriculum aligned with global industry needs.

EMSI doesn’t just prepare engineers. We build futures.
